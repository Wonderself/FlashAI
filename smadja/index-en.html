Regulatory Compliance

Data sovereignty is not merely a competitive advantage. It is a growing legal obligation, both locally and in every market you operate in.

GDPR

The General Data Protection Regulation sets the global standard for personal data processing, storage, and transfer. Any company handling European customer, employee, or partner data must ensure full compliance. Our solutions are designed from the ground up to meet these requirements.

EU AI Act

The European AI Regulation, effective since 2024, classifies AI systems by risk level and imposes transparency, documentation, and governance obligations. If your company serves European clients or partners, anticipating this regulation is a strategic imperative, not an option.

NIS2 Directive

The European cybersecurity directive NIS2 extends security requirements to digital supply chains. Companies integrating European value chains must demonstrate that their systems, including AI-based ones, meet these resilience and protection standards.

Argentine Data Protection

Argentina is one of the few Latin American countries recognized by the EU as offering an adequate level of data protection (Ley 25.326). This adequacy facilitates data transfers with Europe but requires maintaining equivalent standards across your entire digital ecosystem.
