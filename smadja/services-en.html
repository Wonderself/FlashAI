Ethical Framework

The Human Clause

Emmanuel proposes to every client a twelve-month commitment of no layoffs linked to automation, accompanied by training programs for impacted employees. This is not a marketing gesture. It is a structural conviction.

Companies that adopt the Human Clause benefit from a virtuous cycle: social stability reduces turnover costs, employee training strengthens the organization's AI adoption capacity, and the commitment to responsible automation enhances employer branding and ESG positioning.

In jurisdictions with applicable tax incentives (CIR in France, LEC in Argentina), the Human Clause strengthens the compliance dossier and can unlock additional fiscal benefits tied to workforce development and responsible innovation.
