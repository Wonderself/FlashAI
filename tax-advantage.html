Si tu PyME ya está inscrita en el Registro Nacional de Beneficiarios, las facturas de Emmanuel Smadja entran en la base deducible de impuestos a las ganancias con una alícuota efectiva del 10 al 20 % según tamaño. Si tenés Certificado MiPyME vigente y no tenés deudas fiscales exigibles, podés recuperar entre el 35 % y el 100 % del costo de nuestras formaciones IA bajo forma de bono fiscal imputable sobre impuestos nacionales. Tax treaty FAQ

Does my company automatically get tax savings?
Not automatically — your accountant (contador / CPA) must apply the treaty clauses when filing. We provide the compliant Israeli invoice and can connect you to advisors who specialize in Israel treaties. The benefit depends on your country's specific agreement.
What is the difference between FTA and DTA?
FTA (Free Trade Agreement) reduces or eliminates customs duties and tariffs on goods and services. DTA (Double Tax Agreement) prevents the same income from being taxed in both countries, typically by reducing withholding tax rates. Israel has both types with key LATAM markets.
Is there VAT on FlashAI invoices?
No. Israeli export of services is VAT-exempt (0% VAT) under Israeli tax law. You receive a clean invoice with zero VAT, which simplifies your bookkeeping and maximizes the treaty benefit.
My country isn't in the grid — can I still benefit?
Possibly. Israel has 58 DTAs — the grid shows only the most common LATAM + key markets. Send us a WhatsApp and we'll check whether your country has a treaty in under 24 hours.
Can FlashAI handle the tax paperwork for me?
We are not a tax firm — we don't file on your behalf. However, we issue treaty-compliant invoices from our Israeli entity, and we can introduce you to tax advisors who specialize in Israel ↔ LATAM treaties. Your local CPA does the rest.
