Regulatory frameworks we cover

Every FlashAI deployment is designed to meet these standards from day one — not bolted on after.

🇪🇺

GDPR / RGPD

Full data protection compliance. Data minimization, right to erasure, DPO support. EU-hosted models with no US data transfer.

🤖

EU AI Act

Risk classification, transparency obligations, human oversight requirements. We help you document AI systems for high-risk categories.

🏦

DORA

Digital Operational Resilience Act for financial entities. ICT risk management, incident reporting, third-party oversight.

🔒

NIS2

Network and Information Security Directive. Supply chain security, incident response, governance for essential and important entities.

🏥

HDS (Hébergeur de Données de Santé)

French health data hosting certification. Required for any AI processing patient data in France or French-speaking markets.

🇦🇷

Ley 25.326 (Argentina)

Argentine Personal Data Protection Law. Data localization, consent management, cross-border transfer restrictions. Full compliance built in.

How we ensure compliance

01
EU/LATAM hosting only. We deploy on Hetzner (Germany), OVH (France), or your own infrastructure. Zero dependency on US hyperscalers (AWS/GCP/Azure) unless you explicitly choose them.
02
Open-source models. Mistral, DeepSeek, Qwen, Llama — no vendor lock-in, no data sent to OpenAI/Anthropic APIs. Your prompts and data stay on your metal.
03
Encryption at rest & in transit. AES-256 for storage, TLS 1.3 for transport. Key management under your control (BYOK supported).
04
Audit trail & documentation. Every deployment includes a compliance dossier: data flow diagrams, risk assessment, model cards, and incident response procedures.
